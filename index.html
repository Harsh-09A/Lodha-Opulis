Welcome To Lodha Opulis

Lodha Opulis at Kalyan-Shil Road is an exceptional real estate development located in the rapidly growing Palava City, offering luxurious apartments that combine modern architecture with stylish finishes. This thoughtfully designed project boasts world-class amenities, including a vast 1 Lakh sq. ft. clubhouse, a tranquil 2-acre temple space, and 2 acres of beautifully landscaped gardens, ensuring residents have ample opportunities for relaxation and recreation. Top-tier facilities like sports zones, swimming pools, and fitness centers support an active, fulfilling lifestyle. With excellent connectivity to schools, hospitals, shopping centers, and entertainment hubs, Lodha Opulis offers unmatched convenience, while eco-friendly features and smart technologies ensure sustainability. More than just a home, this development fosters a sense of community where families can grow, connect, and create lasting memories, providing the perfect blend of comfort, luxury, and vibrant living.

About Lodha Group

Established in 1980, Lodha Group has emerged as one of the premier real estate developers in Mumbai, renowned for its iconic landmarks and contributions to transforming the city's skyline. Led by an effective team of professionals equipped with technical expertise, extensive cross-functional experience, and excellent domain knowledge, the company has successfully delivered various developments in London, Mumbai, and Pune.

With offices in New York, Dubai, Singapore, and Shanghai, Lodha Group is a multinational corporation that is committed to delivering excellence and quality in all its projects. With a focus on innovation, sustainability, and customer satisfaction, Lodha Group has established a strong reputation as a reliable and trustworthy developer, setting new standards in the real estate industry.
